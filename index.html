Meet Our Team

He is one of the BottomsUp founding members. His dream was to open a coffee shop, and with the start of the pandemic 2yrs ago, that dream changed and grew into a what you see today, a Coffee Tuk Tuk. His aim is to build a coffee brand where customers taste the passion, experience the happiness and enjoy chilled out convos with a cup in hand.

Nashid Cassiem

Behind every great business you will find a strong woman. Meet Shafeeqah, one of the founding members of Bottoms Up Coffee Co. Strong-willed, determined and a businessperson at heart, Shafeeqah is the puppet-master of the business, and is responsible for new business, client services and social media. Somewhat shy and less keen on public speaking she still rules the roost. Being a customer-facing business, she occasionally steps in to assist the Ops team and has recently taken on additional responsibilities of managing the team onsite.

Shafeeqah

Meet Thembela, aka Themz – the man who always has a plan. A father of two, self-taught personal trainer and self-proclaimed “Best Barista in Cape Town”. Themz can pull off anything behind the machine. He says little, does a lot, and has been making coffee for the best part of the last 10yrs. His favorite drink is cranberry juice (wait, whaaaaaat?) and his motto in life is, “don’t warrrrrrrrrry!” But… It… sounds… a lot… like… hakuna? Matata? We’ll go with it
